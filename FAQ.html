  • Kalpabriksha
  • What is Kalpabriksha?

    Kalpabriksha is a youth organization founded by students pursuing higher education in 3 countries; Nepal, India, and The USA.

  • What does Kalpabriksha do?

    In one simple sentence, “Kalpabriksha works with youth for individual and social development.” Kalpabriksha provides opportunities, knowledge, and resources to youth for personal growth. We work with youth from different backgrounds, address their problems, and work together for collaborative solutions.

  • What are the current programs of Kalpabriksha?

    The major program of Kalpabriksha currently is “Change: Because We Can.” “History: Gaze at The Maze”; a workshop on Nepalese History and “Yuwantar”; the annual youth event are other programs. Apart from that, Kalpabriksha conducts different collaborations, seminars, training, and interactions to promote youth development.

  • What is “Change: Because We Can?”

    “Change: Because We Can” is the flagship program of Kalpabriksha. In this program, we reach out to different schools of the country and teach students the concept of Mental Health, Youth and the Internet, the LGBTQA+ community, Harassment and Abuse faced by youth, Toxic Masculinity, and Gender Equality. We use interactive and outdoor learning methods to interact with students and make them understand the aforementioned sensitive issues in a fun yet impactful way.

  • How can I be a part of Kalpabriksha?

    Kalpabriksha constantly hires volunteers for the “Change: Because We Can” campaign. You can fill up the form when we announce the vacancy and based on your form, you will be called for an interview. After you pass the interview, you become a part of Kalpabriksha.

  • How can I be a part of Kalpabriksha executive team?

    You need to be a volunteer in the “Change: Because We Can” campaign first. After working as a volunteer for a certain period of time, you can apply for an executive position.
